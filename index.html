About K Yankee Business Agency

Kofi Yankee Business Agency, established in 2021 and headquartered in Koforidua, Ghana, is a dynamic multi-sector company operating across agricultural, real estate, hospitality, and tourism industry. Founded by visionary entrepreneur Eugene Boateng, our agency is rooted in a passion for sustainability, quality, and innovation.
Our Mission :
To deliver exceptional value to our customers through innovative products and services, built on a foundation of quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction.
Target Market :
Our primary clients include small to medium-sized businesses in the agriculture, real estate, hospitality and tourism sectors, as well as individual investors seeking sustainable and profitable real estate ventures in Ghana.
